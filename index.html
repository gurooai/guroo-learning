FAQ

Frequently asked questions

There's no such thing as too many questions

How does the AI feedback work?

Every assignment you submit is evaluated instantly by our AI, which scores your work against rubrics designed by subject-matter experts. You receive specific, actionable suggestions so you always know what to improve and why. Feedback is immediate; there's no waiting for a human reviewer.

Who's behind the curriculum?

Each program is designed by practitioners who have both the academic pedigree and the real-world track record to back it up. Our curriculum builders are graduates of Harvard, MIT, and Duke, with 10+ years of deep experience in their respective fields. They didn't just study these topics — they've lived them. That's what makes Guroo AI programs genuinely different: the rigor of elite education, applied to the realities of modern professional life.

What does self-paced actually mean here?

Self-paced means you're in control. Each program comes with a recommended completion window to help you stay on track and get the most out of the material — but it's exactly that: a recommendation, not a requirement. You're free to move through modules at whatever speed works for your schedule, revisit content, and explore areas that are most relevant to your work. The structure is there to guide you, not constrain you.

How is Guroo AI different from other online learning platforms?

Most platforms prioritize content delivery — passive video courses you watch and quickly forget. Guroo AI is application-first: every concept is immediately put to work using your real business context. Combined with AI-powered feedback on every submission and cohort visibility, you build measurable capability, not just completion certificates.
